Origami designs:

Camel

Interesting facts about camel:

  • There are two types of camels: One humped or “dromedary” camels and two humped Bactrian camels.
  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.

Chameleon

Interesting facts about chameleon:

  • Chameleons change their skin color to communicate or regulate body temperature.
  • Almost half of all chameleons live in Madagascar.

Cicada

Interesting facts about cicada:

  • Male cicadas make a chorus of chirping at night.
  • Cicadas live in hibernation underground for most of their lives.

Panda

Interesting facts about panda:

  • Panda live a lazy life - they just eat and sleep a lot.
  • Pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.

Pigeon

Interesting facts about pigeon:

  • Pidgeons are the oldest domesticated birds.
  • They were once used as messengers to send parchment over large distances.

Teddy

Interesting facts about teddy:

  • Teddy bears got their name from the story that Teddy Roosevelt refused to shoot a bear cub while on a hunting trip in 1902.
  • The first toy stuffed bear was created by German toymaker Margarete Steiff in 1903.
